Interactive Chat Bot (Ganga Program)

Our project focuses on creating an AI, ML, and chatbot-powered interactive Robot Mascot named "Chacha Chaudhary", the mascot of the Namami Gange Programme. This independent robot aims to connect with school children, the common man, and all stakeholders of the National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) to create awareness and disseminate information. The robot, equipped with a touch panel, will guide visitors through each component of the Namami Gange flagship program in the River Basin War Room & Ganga Museum. Additionally, a digital avatar of Chacha Chaudhary will be available on the NMCG website, providing an interactive platform to impart information, awareness, and education about riverine ecology. The solution is designed to be user-friendly and citizen-centric, enhancing the river people's connection component of the Namami Gange.
